A Little Bit About Me

Christoper Ian de Alday

Hello, my name is Ian. I have been working in the IT industry for the past five years and most recently served as an Assistant IT Manager at SMX Convention Center under SM Group of Companies. In this role, I was responsible for ensuring that all the systems, servers, file servers, web servers, firewalls, switches, CCTV, Wi-Fi, and local area networks were running efficiently.

Additionally, I was responsible for providing technical support to employees, answering their queries, and troubleshooting software and hardware issues. I also had the opportunity to handle project management tasks, such as setting up and configuring devices for new branches and monitoring implementation to ensure successful execution.

One of the exciting moments of my career was when I had to develop a website to monitor people inside the venue in real-time due to safety protocols during the pandemic. This project sparked my interest in web development, and I decided to learn more about building websites. That's why I enrolled in a reputable boot camp last year and took a 3-4 months Full Stack Web Developer Course and recently graduated this February 28, 2023.

During my training, I gained experience using front-end tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Bootstrap, Tailwind and React. For my backend development I gained experience on developing using Node.Js and Express.JS.

After my graduation from Zuitt, I continued to pursue my passion for programming and enrolled in a short course to learn Python Basic Syntax, Python OOP, MySQL, and frameworks such as Django. This additional training allowed me to expand my knowledge and skills further that makes me more competitive in the industry, and I'm eager to apply these skills to contribute to your company's success.
